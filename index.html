ABOUT US

Year of establishment: 1993

Nature of business: Manufacturer and supplier

Legal status of firm: Proprietor

Established in the year 1994, we, Das Industries, are leading manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Drill Pipe Wiper, Elevator Balancing Strap, Kelley Wiper & Mud Pump Spares. The offered array of these products are manufactured by our experts using premium quality raw material and advanced machines. We are constantly looking forward to upgrade our technology and incorporate advanced systems for manufacturing components & accessories for Oil Rig Explorations.

Moreover, a quality control department continuously monitors the quality of these products on the basis of some stringent parameters. We have a team of diligent professionals, which helps us in offering best quality products to the customers. We subject our products to quality control tests based on several parameters and take necessary quality measures. Our expert team comprises of best professionals who endeavor to provide quality products for the players to get the full return of their money. We are highly principled and believe in engaging in ethical business dealings only. Our exporter countries are East Asia, Middle East, South East Asia and Indian Subcontinent.
